This repository documents the annual course **Intelligent Robotics** offered by the **University of Vaasa (Finland)**

Unlike traditional robotics courses, this course focuses on **intelligent robots** capable of perception, decision-making, and autonomous behavior similar to the difference between conventional vehicles and self-driving cars

---

## Course Overview

The course integrates concepts from:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Control Systems
- Sensors and Sensor Fusion
- Decision Making
- Motion Planning
- Autonomous Navigation

The goal is to transform robots from simple command-executing machines into **intelligent systems that understand and interact with their environment**

---

## Main Topics

- Locomotion and Kinematics
- Robot Perception
- Sensor Fusion
- Localization and Navigation
- Decision Making and Motion Planning
- AI Applications in Modern Robotics

---

## Practical Components

- Industrial visit to **ABB Robotics Factory**
- Interactive on-campus lectures
- Online participation for international students
- Project: Designing a robot for specialized environments
- Hands-on exercises and final exam

---

## Course Format

- Hybrid delivery (on-campus & online)
- Students from Finland and abroad
- Live interactive discussions
